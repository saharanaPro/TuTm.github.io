Mission Statement

At the Tiger U Tiger Me
Foundation

We understand that early education and child development are essential components in producing self-sufficient and thriving individuals. The mission of the Tiger U Tiger Me foundation is to promote the advancement of our community by providing educational leadership through life essential resources. Although we aim to serve everyone in our community, our target population are families with student-athletes who are facing socioeconomic hardships. The Tiger U Tiger Me foundation will provide support to our community in areas that encompass, but are not limited to, food – with an emphasis on diet and nutrition education, shelter, clothing, utilities, academics, agriculture, college recruiting processes, performance testing for student-athletes, and college tour travel fees. In addition to this support, one of our main goals is to provide services and resources that aid in the professional development of students at secondary levels of education.

The Tiger U Tiger Me (TUTM) Foundation

Is committed to providing support for the development of life skills and educational leadership in the local sports community for minorities. However, we also see the basic need for shelter, nutritional intervention, the ability to provide a safe environment and the importance of developing social emotional wellness within our youth.
